  • The Photographer

    The name is Joshua Espallardo. He is a freelance photographer and a graduate of Bachelor of Arts in Multimedia Studies at University of the Philippines Open University. He aims to build a name for himself in the photography industry. He likes traveling around the world, bringing his camera with him, to capture the essence of life.

    "Capturing moments of life through the lens of my camera, helps me to remember memories that otherwise, would have been long forgotten already. It is important for me to relive these fun moments, to remind me that no matter how hard it is, there is always beauty to be seen and experienced in life."
